Nachos Recipe

Quick and Simple Doritos Nachos

Craving a snack for you or a party?

These quick and easy Dorito nachos are sure to make you or your guests say WOW!

Ingredients

  • 330g orininal corn chips
  • 1 cup medium salsa
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1/4 tbsp group chili
  • 2 tbsp groud cumin seed
  • 2 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 can red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1.5/2 cup cheddar cheese, shredded

Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180C. Arrange half of the corn chips on an 11 or 12-inch ovenproof platter or pizza pan. Set aside.
  2. In a large skillet cook onion and ground beef over medium heat until brown. Drain off fat.
  3. Add in the cumin, paprika and chilli, cook for 2 minutes
  4. Stir the red kidney beans and the 1 cup salsa into the beef.
  5. Cook for a further 5 minutes.
  6. Spoon half of the mixture over chips. Sprinkle with half the cheese. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes or until cheese just melts.
  7. Remove from oven. Top with remaining chips, beef mixture, and cheese. Bake for a further 10 minutes or until cheese has melted
  8. Top with your selected additional toppings and medium salsa. Optional toppings: Thinly sliced green onion, snipped fresh cilantro, seeded and sliced fresh jalapeno chilli pepper, sour cream, guacamole and additional medium salsa.
