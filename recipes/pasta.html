Very simple Pasta with olive oil and garlic

Are you in a pinch? Want something nutritious to eat but don't have the time?

Well this is the perfect garlic pasta recipe for you. With only a total prep and cook time of 15 minutes, you'll be on your way before you even know it!

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 1 tablesppoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Warm the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat
  2. Cook the garlic in the oil until lightly golden and softened. Keep an eye on it, as its brownns relatively fast.
  3. Reserve a tablespoon of the starchy pasta water before draining the pasta
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti, aloing with the tablespoon of starchy pasta water, to the skillet and toss it with the garlic oil
  5. Add chopped parsley and Parmesan cheese
  6. Toss it all again and serve!
